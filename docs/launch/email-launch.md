## Email Design Notes

- **Plain text preferred** for Indie Hackers / developer audience
- **Single column**, max 600px width
- **Clear CTAs**: One primary, one secondary per email
- **No images in email body** (spam filters). Link to landing page for visuals.
- **Signature**: Name + GitHub link + "Reply to this email — I read every one"

## Sending Schedule

| Email | Day | Time (GMT) | Audience |

| Reminder | Day 3 | 10:00 | Full list |
| Last Chance | Day 7 | 09:00 | Non-buyers only |

## Segmentation

- **Buyers**: Remove from sequence after purchase. Add to "Customers" list.
- **Free tier users**: Send Email 2 and 3, but emphasise "upgrade when ready" not urgency.
- **Non-openers**: Resend with new subject line after 48 hours.
