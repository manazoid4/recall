## Reply Tweet 1 (FAQ — LLM Key)

Q: Do I need an LLM key?

A: For AI enrichment and semantic search, yes. For full-text search and basic boards, no. Your key, your cost. Typical use is pennies per hundred items. That's the tradeoff that makes £49 lifetime possible.
