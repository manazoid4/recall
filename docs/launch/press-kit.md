## The Story

### The Problem

The average knowledge worker has saved content scattered across 5+ platforms — Instagram, YouTube, X, TikTok, Reddit, Pocket. Most of it is never seen again. Bookmarks and native "save" features are digital graveyards: content goes in, nothing comes out.

### The Solution

Recall pulls saved content from every platform into a single, searchable knowledge base. AI enrichment generates summaries, tags, and extracts entities. Semantic search finds content by meaning — not just keywords. A knowledge graph surfaces connections between saves the user didn't know existed.

### The Differentiator

**Local-first by design.** Your data lives in a JSON file on your machine. No cloud lock-in, no data mining, no acquisition risk. The only external calls are to your own LLM provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc) — your key, your cost, your control.

**Pricing that respects users.** £49 lifetime. No subscriptions. No feature gates. The "bring your own LLM key" model means zero inference cost to the developer — that's how lifetime pricing works without burning cash.

## Key Facts

- **Import sources:** Instagram, YouTube, X, TikTok, Reddit, Pocket (more coming)
- **AI features:** Auto-summaries, tag generation, entity extraction, sentiment analysis
- **Search:** Semantic (vector) search + full-text search
- **Knowledge graph:** Force-directed visualisation of connected saves
- **Export:** Obsidian markdown with wikilinks and frontmatter
- **Chrome extension:** Auto-sync from Instagram and X as you save
- **Shareable boards:** Public URLs for curated collections
- **Free tier:** 50 items, full-text search, manual import, 1 board
- **Pro tier:** Unlimited items, AI search, AI enrichment, knowledge graph, unlimited boards, Chrome extension, Obsidian export, priority support
